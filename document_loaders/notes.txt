
Artificial Intelligence Notes

Introduction
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science focused on creating systems
that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence.

Applications of AI
1. Recommendation Systems
2. Chatbots
3. Self-driving Cars
4. Medical Diagnosis
5. Fraud Detection

Machine Learning
Machine Learning is a subset of AI where systems learn patterns from data.

Types of Machine Learning
- Supervised Learning
- Unsupervised Learning
- Reinforcement Learning

Natural Language Processing
NLP allows computers to understand and generate human language.

Conclusion
AI is transforming industries and creating new opportunities in technology.
